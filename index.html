Story of Visonary Jewelry


Visionary Jewelry is a fine jewel web store founded by Jiaxin Li and her friends from her high school and her university. Website made by Gongxia Chen.

Jiaxin Li began her business by opening a physical jewelry shop and creating her online shop in China. When she get into Washington University in St. Louis, she decided to also bring her business "Visionary Jewel" to the US.
